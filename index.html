ABOUT ME
My name is Calvin House. I'm from Christchurch, studying graphic design at Massey University in Wellington. I've been involved with art for three years now. Strong influences of mine are Kieth Haring's cartoon style and Jean-Michel Basquiat's creativity throughout many of his works that defined the 80's art scene.
I'ved recently sold six paintings at an art auction in Christchurch which gave me the inspiration to do some commission work for friends and family. From there, I've done some freelance design work for Annam restaurant and Modica Group.
Common themes in my art are the use of cartoon themes and images.
